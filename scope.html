Scope

The PRINCESS-DIVA Trial aims to compare the incidence of complication rates between two common vascular access devices used for Difficult Intravenous Access (DIVA) patients.

Specifically, the trial evaluates catheter-related complications in patients randomized to receive either a Midline catheter or a Central venous catheter.
